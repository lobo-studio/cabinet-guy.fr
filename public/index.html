A graduate of the University of Lyon III and the IAE of Strasbourg, Maitre Guy Ghislaine has been a lawyer at the Bar of Fort-de-France since 1993. After several experiences as a collaborating lawyer in Parisian and Martinique law firms, she practices Today in his own practice. Former member of the Council of the Order, his activity revolves around private law. A member of the Association of Real Estate Lawyers, she works in real estate law and is responsible for the sale and management of real estate.
